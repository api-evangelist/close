Close has launched Chloe, an AI voice agent integrated directly into their CRM platform for U.S. customers that automates lead qualification, meeting booking, and follow-ups while logging information automatically. According to Close's leadership, Chloe addresses a persistent customer challenge: there were more opportunities they wanted to pursue than they had time to work.
